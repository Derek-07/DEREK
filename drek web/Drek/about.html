Our Story

DREK was born in Agra with a simple idea — make professional digital services available to everyone. We started as a small team of passionate developers and designers, and today we have grown into a full-service digital agency.

We believe that every business, no matter how big or small, deserves a strong online presence. That is why we offer everything from website development and graphic design to video editing, 3D modeling, and even professional training courses.

Our team uses the best tools in the industry — Figma for UI/UX design, Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator for graphics, Premiere Pro and After Effects for video editing, and Maya and Blender for 3D work. On the development side, we work with React, Next.js, Node.js, Python, and more.
